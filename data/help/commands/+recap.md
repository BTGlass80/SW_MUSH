Show your narrative recap: a summary of your character's background,
recent in-game actions, and active personal quests. The Director AI
uses this information to generate story hooks tailored specifically
to you.

SYNTAX

  +recap

WHAT IS INCLUDED

  - Your character background (set via +background)
  - A digest of your recent in-game activity
  - Active personal quests (see +quests)

HOW THE DIRECTOR USES IT

  When the Director AI generates scene hooks, NPC dialogue, or
  personal quest offers, it reads your recap to make the content
  relevant to your character's history and goals. Keeping your
  +background up to date makes Director-generated content more
  personal and immersive.

EXAMPLES

  +recap
  → Displays your full recap.

SEE ALSO

  +quests      Your active personal quests.
  +background  Set or view your character backstory.
  +scenes      Browse your logged scenes.

CHEAT SHEET
  +recap   show your narrative recap
