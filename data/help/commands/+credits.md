Display your current credit balance — the amount of galactic
standard credits your character currently holds.

SYNTAX

  +credits
  credits
  balance
  wallet

All four forms are equivalent.

WHAT ARE CREDITS?

  Credits are the universal currency of the galaxy. You earn them
  through missions, bounties, salvage, smuggling runs, intel
  handovers, and other activities. You spend them at shops,
  the commissary, on ship repairs, insurance, and crew wages.

  The +finances command gives a full income/expense breakdown.

EXAMPLES

  +credits
  → Credits: 4,250

SEE ALSO

  +finances    Detailed credit history, pay schedule, and expenses.
  +shop        Browse available items to purchase.
  +commissary  Faction gear requisition.

CHEAT SHEET
  +credits / credits / balance / wallet   show credit balance
