Display your Force status: Control, Sense, and Alter ratings,
current Force Points, and any accumulated Dark Side Points.

SYNTAX

  +forcestatus
  forcestatus
  fstatus

WHAT IS SHOWN

  Control     Your dice rating for active Force use and
              resisting the dark side.
  Sense       Your ability to perceive the Force, read others,
              and detect threats.
  Alter       Your ability to manipulate the physical world
              through the Force.
  Force Points   Renewable resource spent to enhance rolls or
                  power certain abilities. Recovering FP requires
                  rest and meditation.
  Dark Side Points   Accumulated from unethical Force use.
                      Rising DSP darkens your character's arc.

You must be Force-sensitive to have a meaningful readout here.
Non-Force-sensitive characters will see "You are not Force-sensitive."

EXAMPLES

  +forcestatus
  → Control: 3D+1  Sense: 2D  Alter: 2D+2
    Force Points: 2  Dark Side Points: 0

SEE ALSO

  +powers      List available and locked Force powers.
  +meditate    Reduce Weight of War at the Jedi Temple (uses 1 FP).
  +sheet       Your full character sheet.

CHEAT SHEET
  +forcestatus / fstatus   show Force attributes and points
