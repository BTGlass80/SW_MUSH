Display the Galactic News Network bulletin board — a feed of recent
Director-authored world events reflecting the state of the Clone Wars galaxy.
Entries are generated automatically as the Director AI reacts to player
actions and faction territory shifts.

SYNTAX

  +news
  news

CONTENT

  The news board covers:
  • Territory changes and faction conflicts (Clone Wars events)
  • Regional stability shifts driven by player actions
  • Major economic or political events generated by the Director
  • Seasonal or campaign-arc announcements by staff

  Up to 10 recent entries are shown. Older news scrolls off the board.

NOTES

  • News entries are IC — they represent broadcasts heard across the HoloNet.
  • +holonet lets you post your own HoloNet messages (player-driven posts).
  • The board may be empty if the Director has not generated events yet.

EXAMPLES

  +news
  → Read the latest galactic headlines.

CHEAT SHEET
  +news      = Director-authored world-event feed
  +holonet   = player-posted HoloNet messages
