Drink from a carried water canteen to recover from **dehydration**, the
debuff inflicted by prolonged exposure to desert heat (Tatooine and other
arid zones).

SYNTAX
  drink
  hydrate

NOTES
  - You must be carrying a **water canteen** to drink. Without one, find a
    canteen or leave the heat to recover.
  - Dehydration is no longer permanent: it also decays on its own once you
    leave the hazard, so you are never permanently stuck — drinking simply
    clears it instantly.
  - Other environmental hazards (e.g. toxic exposure) follow the same rule:
    they fade once you leave the source.
