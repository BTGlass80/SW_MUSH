## At a glance

**Mustafar** is the **lava planet of the Outer Rim** — a volcanic, mineral-rich world where 35% of the surface is liquid lava, the Mustafarian inhabitants live in subterranean and shielded surface settlements, and the Techno Union and Black Sun both maintain operations during the Clone Wars. Most importantly, it is canonically the site of two pivotal events:

1. **The Mustafar massacre (19 BBY).** The Separatist Council retreats here at war's end expecting peace negotiations with the new Empire. Vader (newly-fallen Anakin) arrives instead and kills them all on Sidious's orders.
2. **The Vader-Obi-Wan duel (19 BBY, immediately after).** The era-ending duel that maims Anakin, leaves him for dead beside a lava river, and ends the Clone Wars era. Sidious recovers him; he is rebuilt as the Vader we know.

For SW_MUSH purposes, Mustafar is **a Force-resonant landmark candidate** because of the Vader-Obi-Wan duel's emotional weight, **a CIS infrastructure location** during the war, and **a tightly-controlled fixed campaign clock event location** at war's end.

## Geography and physical character

- **Location:** Outer Rim Territories, grid square L-19, on the Tosste Spur trade route off the Rimma Trade Route.
- **Composition:** Young volcanic planet. 35% of the surface is liquid lava. Mineral-rich — valuable allotropes surface from the planetary core. **Bronzium** is one of the notable extracted metals; Mustafarian mines supply it galaxy-wide.
- **Atmosphere:** Hot, ash-filled, sulfurous. Breathable for most species but uncomfortable; respirators recommended for long stays.
- **Wildlife:** Lava-adapted creatures including **lava fleas** (rideable mounts the Mustafarians use), **fireworms**, and other heat-tolerant fauna.
- **Cosmological note:** Some in-universe spiritual traditions believed Mustafar's volatile state was driven by spiritual forces. The lava-and-fire imagery canonically symbolizes (per George Lucas's intent) Biblical hell — the world's design is meant to externalize the moral collapse that happens upon it.

## Inhabitants

- **Mustafarians:** The native sentient species. Lava-adapted physiology; live in subterranean and shielded surface communities. Two subspecies in canon: the taller Northern Mustafarians (industrial workforce) and the smaller Southern Mustafarians (clerical/management).
- **Mining and refinery operations** form the planet's economic base. The Techno Union (Wat Tambor) operates major facilities here.

## Clone Wars-era status

- **Political alignment:** **CIS-aligned**, but the Mustafarians joined the Confederacy primarily for the contract revenue rather than ideological commitment. Life on the planet continues largely unchanged through the war; the Mustafarians are pragmatic.
- **Black Sun operations:** During the Clone Wars, **Black Sun** (the Falleen-led criminal syndicate) maintains a major operations base on Mustafar. The remoteness and ineffective Republic patrolling of the Outer Rim let organized crime flourish here. The Black Sun leadership is canonically based on Mustafar at one point.
- **Shadow Collective takeover:** During Maul's rise, **Savage Opress** travels to Mustafar and beheads the Black Sun leadership during failed negotiations; **Ziton Moj** becomes the new leader and aligns Black Sun with Maul's Shadow Collective. After this, Black Sun on Mustafar is a Shadow Collective asset.
- **Sidious Force-sensitive children facility:** Sidious's anonymous bounty hunter contract (executed by Cad Bane) intended to deliver kidnapped Force-sensitive infants to a **Mustafar facility** for processing into Force-using Sith spies. The facility was destroyed when Republic forces approached; the children were extracted by Anakin and Ahsoka.

## Pivotal end-of-war events (19 BBY)

These events are **fixed campaign clock anchors** for the era's end. The Director AI must treat them as canonical and immovable.

### 1. Separatist Council massacre
With Order 66 in execution and the Galactic Empire being declared, the surviving Separatist Council members (Nute Gunray, Wat Tambor, San Hill, Shu Mai, Passel Argente, Poggle the Lesser, etc.) retreat to a Techno Union complex on Mustafar expecting peace negotiations with the Empire. Sidious sends **Darth Vader** (newly-christened Anakin) to kill them all and deactivate the droid army. Vader carries out the executions with a lightsaber. The CIS political leadership ends here.

### 2. The Vader-Obi-Wan duel
Following the Council massacre, **Obi-Wan Kenobi** arrives (smuggled aboard Padmé Amidala's ship, hidden until landing). Padmé attempts to reason with Anakin; he Force-chokes her in fury when Obi-Wan reveals himself; Padmé survives but is gravely wounded. Anakin and Obi-Wan duel through the Techno Union complex and across the lava streams. The duel ends beside a lava river: Obi-Wan wins, severing Anakin's remaining limbs; Anakin's body is incinerated by the lava but he survives. Obi-Wan takes Anakin's lightsaber, leaves him for dead, and departs with Padmé. Sidious arrives shortly after; recovers the dying Anakin; rebuilds him on Coruscant in the iconic black armor.

### Cultural memory after the duel
Stories of the duel circulate among Mustafarians for years afterward, though no official record is kept. Obi-Wan canonically remembers the lava-river image as a recurring traumatic memory throughout his Tatooine exile. The site becomes Force-resonant by virtue of what happened there.

## Imperial Era developments (out of CW project scope, but useful context)

- **Fortress Vader / Vader's Castle.** Built by Vader during the Imperial Era atop a Sith cave on Mustafar (with the dark-side Sith Lord Momin's design assistance). Used as Vader's personal sanctum.
- **Imperial protection.** Mustafar becomes a Sith-protected world; Imperial Star Destroyers patrol the system; the Empire treats the planet as effectively Vader's domain.
- **Pilgrimages.** After Vader's death, dark-side acolytes pilgrimage to the ruins of Fortress Vader. The **Alazmec of Winsit** are a canonical Vader-venerating cult that defends Mustafar's secrets in the New Republic Era.
- **Vader's aide Vaneé** lives at the castle for decades, eventually attempting to ritually create a new Vader from Kylo Ren before being killed.

## Use in `clone_wars_director_lore_pivot_design_v1.md`

The Director AI must:

- **Treat the Mustafar massacre and Vader-Obi-Wan duel as fixed end-of-war events.** Players cannot prevent them; they happen at the war's end regardless of player action. The campaign clock should preserve these.
- **Allow Mustafar visits during the war for industrial/criminal/CIS content.** Pre-Council-massacre, Mustafar is a working CIS-aligned mining world where multiple factions operate.
- **Treat the Mustafarians as pragmatic, not villainous.** They aligned with the CIS for contracts; they are not ideological enemies of the Republic. Republic-side players visiting Mustafar should encounter civilians who are wary but not hostile.
- **Treat the lava environment as canonically punishing.** Players should require respirators, heat-resistant gear, or vehicle protection. The environment itself is an antagonist.
