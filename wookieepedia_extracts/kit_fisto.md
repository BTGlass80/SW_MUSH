## At a glance

Kit Fisto is the **Nautolan member of the Jedi High Council** — green-skinned, head-tentacled, ever-smiling, deeply skilled. Native of **Glee Anselm**, a primarily aquatic Mid Rim world. Designated a **Jedi Vanguard** (combat-specialist Master class). One of four Council members who accompany Mace Windu to arrest Sidious in the office; among the first three killed in that confrontation.

Tonally: warm, calm, observant. The Director AI must voice him as **the Council member with the easiest smile** — Kit Fisto is the closest thing to "approachable" the High Council has, the Jedi most likely to laugh in informal settings, the one whose presence puts younger Jedi at ease.

## Identity and rank progression

- **Species:** Nautolan — green-skinned, fourteen head-tentacles (lekku-like sensory organs that can detect pheromones in water and air), large dark eyes. Aquatic-adapted; can breathe both air and water; outstanding underwater swimmer.
- **Homeworld:** Glee Anselm — primarily aquatic Mid Rim world. Nautolans share the planet with the **Anselmi** (a non-Force-sensitive species that has occasional ethnic tensions with the Nautolans; one canonical Anselmi-Nautolan conflict over a stolen statue triggered Kit Fisto's diplomatic exclusion from peace talks).
- **Knighthood and Mastery:** Long-tenured Jedi Master before the Clone Wars.
- **Council seat:** High Council member throughout the war.
- **Padawan:** **Nahdar Vebb** — Mon Calamari Force-sensitive who Kit Fisto trained to Knighthood. Killed by Grievous in Grievous's lair; Kit Fisto witnessed Vebb's death and barely survived their joint pursuit of Grievous.
- **Designations:** Jedi Vanguard (combat-specialist Master); Jedi General in the GAR.
- **Death:** 19 BBY, in Palpatine's office during the Council attempt to arrest Sidious. Killed early in the duel; Mace Windu outlasts the rest of the arrest party.

## Voice and personality (for Director AI rendering)

### Core register
- **Warm, observant, easy-smiling.** Kit Fisto is canonically the most casually friendly High Council member. The Director AI should let warmth surface in his interactions across both ranks and species.
- **Calm authority.** When he speaks, he is heard. But he doesn't push for attention.
- **Clever and analytical.** Canonically observant in ways other Jedi miss — discerns hidden mechanisms, notices small details, "a second look is usually rewarding." Useful for investigative content.
- **Iconic verbal patterns:** Kit Fisto's actual canonical quotes are sparse; the Director AI has latitude to construct dialogue that fits his warm-observant register.

### Modulations under specific pressures
- **In combat:** Master swordsman; survives the First Battle of Geonosis when many Jedi don't; holds his own against multiple opponents simultaneously. The Director AI should let him be combat-confident without being aggressive.
- **In aquatic combat:** Specialty mode. Nautolan biology makes him uniquely capable underwater. Mon Cala campaign is his showcase.
- **With Padawans (current and former):** Patient, encouraging, generous with praise. Kit Fisto is canonically the kind of Master who builds confidence in apprentices.
- **With Aayla Secura and Saesee Tiin (canonical close associates):** Easy collegial warmth. The Director AI should let scenes among these three feel relaxed.
- **In the Sidious arrest:** Grim resolve. He understands the stakes; he goes anyway.

## Locations the Director AI may place him

- **Coruscant — Jedi Temple, Council Chambers.** His default during Council sessions.
- **Mon Cala / Dac.** Mid-war underwater campaign (Battle of Mon Cala) — his canonical showcase deployment with SCUBA-trooper battalion under Commander Monnk.
- **Grievous's lair** (various Outer Rim locations) — pursued Grievous after Vebb's death; the trophy chamber encounter is his canonical engagement.
- **Glee Anselm.** Homeworld; rarely visits during the war but available for diplomatic / homeworld content.
- **Palpatine's office (Coruscant Senate District).** Site of his death during the Sidious arrest.

## Combat capabilities (lore-only)

- **Lightsaber form: Form I (Shii-Cho).** The basic, simplest of the lightsaber forms — Kit Fisto is canonically a master of the foundational style, demonstrating that "simple" doesn't mean "weak." His Form I is exceptional.
- **Lightsaber:** Single hilt; **green blade**.
- **Force aptitude:** Standard Master-level — telekinesis, mind tricks, Force jump, Force barrier. **Nautolan pheromone-sensing** is a biological gift that augments his Force perception, particularly underwater where pheromones travel readily.
- **Underwater combat specialty.** Nautolans are aquatic-adapted; Kit Fisto fights as effectively underwater as on land. The Director AI should let him be the era's foremost aquatic-combat Jedi.
- **Pilot:** Competent.

## Use in `clone_wars_director_lore_pivot_design_v1.md`

The Director AI must:

- **Voice him with warm friendliness.** Don't make him stoic or grim. Kit Fisto is the smiling Council member.
- **Treat him as available for aquatic content.** When player content involves water, swimming, ocean planets, underwater combat — Kit Fisto is the canonical Jedi for that environment.
- **Treat his death (Palpatine's office, 19 BBY) as a fixed campaign clock event.** End of the war.
- **Allow him as a warm Council figure for Padawan PCs.** Players who need encouragement from a Council member can find it from Kit Fisto where they would not from Mace Windu or Ki-Adi-Mundi.

## Use in `padawan_master_system_design_v1.md`

Kit Fisto is the canonical case for **the encouraging Master archetype** — generous with praise, patient with mistakes, focused on building Padawan confidence rather than enforcing strict orthodoxy. Pattern observation:

- **Master with a Padawan who dies in service.** Nahdar Vebb's death at Grievous's hands is a canonical "the Padawan dies before completing training" scenario. The system should support this outcome with appropriate narrative weight.
- **Master who continues teaching peers after the Padawan's death.** Kit Fisto continues to support Ahsoka and other young Jedi after Vebb's loss; the teaching reflex remains.
