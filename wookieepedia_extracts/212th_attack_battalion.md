## At a glance

The **212th Attack Battalion** is **General Obi-Wan Kenobi's command unit** — sister legion to the 501st in fame and operational tempo, but with a distinctly different culture. Serves under the **7th Sky Corps** within the Grand Army of the Republic. Led on the clone side by **Marshal Commander Cody (CC-2224)**. Distinctive **orange markings** on Phase I and Phase II clone trooper armor.

For SW_MUSH purposes, the 212th is the **canonical "Obi-Wan's troops" reference unit** — paired with the 501st for joint operations across many CW arcs, with a culture that mirrors Kenobi's character: disciplined, professional, high-morale, less casual than the 501st's "Anakin's beloved unit" warmth.

## Composition and identity

- **Formal designation:** 212th Attack Battalion, 7th Sky Corps, Grand Army of the Republic.
- **Alternate names:** 212th Republic Battalion, the 212th Battalion.
- **Markings:** **Orange trim** on white armor — mirrors Kenobi's lightsaber color (his blade is blue, but the orange is the Battalion's identifying scheme distinct from the 501st blue).
- **Subordinate units:**
  - **212th Recon Division** — special detachment for reconnaissance operations.
  - **Foxtrot Group** — named subordinate unit.
  - **Ghost Company** — named subordinate company within the battalion.
- **Non-clone integration:** A full company of **Roonian** military defense forces was transferred to the 212th during the early CW for the **Battle of Abrion Bridge**, where Kenobi, Cody, and Roonian Commander **Mekedrix** led a combined clone-and-Roonian force. This is canonical proof that non-clone soldiers served in GAR units.

## Notable Clone Wars deployments

- **First Battle of Geonosis (22 BBY).** The Battalion's birth as a fighting force.
- **Battle of Christophsis (early CW).** Joint operation with the 501st under Anakin/Rex.
- **Battle of Teth.** Hutt-related campaign.
- **Battle of Ryloth.** The 212th lands with Kenobi and the 7th Sky Corps to liberate the Twi'lek homeworld; the **Nabat hostage rescue** is a canonical 212th operation where Kenobi insists the Twi'lek civilians used as droid living-shields be rescued before the proton cannons are destroyed.
- **Siege of Hisseen.** Joint operation with the 501st (Anakin/Rex) and the 104th Battalion (Plo Koon/Wolffe). Multi-Jedi multi-unit Republic operation.
- **Battle of Abrion Bridge.** Roonian-integrated joint operation.
- **Second Battle of Geonosis.** Joint operation; 212th assists in droid factory destruction and Poggle the Lesser's capture.
- **Battle of Umbara.** Joint operation with the 501st. The 212th and 501st together arrest **fallen Jedi General Pong Krell** for treason — Krell had been deliberately killing his own clones to undermine the war effort and signal his shift toward the dark side.
- **Battle of Utapau (19 BBY).** **Final CW deployment.** The 212th lands with Kenobi to engage Grievous's forces. Kenobi kills Grievous in personal combat. Then **Commander Cody receives Order 66 from Sidious** and orders the Battalion to fire on Kenobi's varactyl mount. Kenobi survives by falling into a deep grotto and escaping.

## Voice and culture (for Director AI rendering)

The 212th's culture is **disciplined-professional with quiet pride**, distinct from the 501st's personal-Anakin-loyalty culture.

- **Discipline first.** The 212th runs a tight ship. Drill, paperwork, standard operating procedure are visibly observed. The Director AI should let 212th troopers be more formal in speech than 501st troopers.
- **Pride in their general.** Troopers respect Kenobi's reputation as "the Negotiator." They appreciate that he tries to talk problems down before fighting them.
- **Multi-unit cooperation muscle memory.** The 212th and 501st have worked together so often they coordinate naturally; mutual professional respect.
- **Cody specifically.** Marshal Commander Cody's voice register is calm, measured, professionally devoted to Kenobi. The Director AI should voice him as the canonical "by-the-book commander who genuinely respects his Jedi General."

## Use in `weight_of_war_design_v1.md`

The 212th is the **second canonical line-unit reference** for Republic-side player content (paired with the 501st):

- **Squad-scale player party content.** Players running 212th troopers should encounter the unit's culture: discipline, professionalism, deep respect for Kenobi specifically, less personal warmth than the 501st but high mutual respect within the unit.
- **Mass-combat unit reference.** A "212th Attack Battalion" mass-combat unit could be templated using `cwcg_extraction_v1.md` §2 (Clone Trooper Battalion sample). The Battalion is composed of multiple companies; mass-combat unit construction would represent it as several stacked units.
- **Joint operations with the 501st.** Many canonical operations involve both units. Player content can use this template for combined-arms scenarios.
- **The Cody-Kenobi inflection point.** Order 66 at Utapau. The most heart-wrenching of the canonical Order 66 betrayals because Cody's loyalty to Kenobi was canonically real. Players in late-CW 212th content must reckon with this looming pivot.

## Use in `clone_wars_director_lore_pivot_design_v1.md`
