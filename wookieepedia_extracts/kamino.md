## At a glance

**Kamino** is the **clone-army factory world** — an isolated ocean planet beyond the Outer Rim, hidden from the Republic until Obi-Wan Kenobi traced Jango Fett there in 22 BBY (Dooku had erased Kamino from the Jedi Archives). Home to the **Kaminoans**, a tall, slender, near-amphibious species who specialize in genetic engineering and large-scale cloning. The clone army that becomes the Grand Army of the Republic is grown, trained, and eventually inhibitor-chipped here. Throughout the Clone Wars, Kamino is one of the Republic's most strategically vital and most aggressively defended assets.

For SW_MUSH purposes, Kamino is the **canonical clone origin location** — central to any clone-trooper player content, the site of the inhibitor chip conspiracy, and Shaak Ti's primary station throughout the war.

## Geography and physical character

- **Location:** Beyond the Outer Rim, in the Wild Space borderlands (galactic coordinates erased from Jedi records). Specifically: in the **Kamino system** at the edge of charted space.
- **Composition:** Ocean planet — entire surface is water, with violent storms a near-constant atmospheric feature. **Cities are constructed on stilts** above the wave lines, designed to withstand the storms.
- **Climate:** Storm-driven. Heavy rain, lightning, high winds. Indoor environments are sterile, climate-controlled, dimly lit.
- **Capital:** **Tipoca City** — the planetary capital and primary cloning facility. Iconic stilted-platform architecture; multi-domed; clone training grounds, barracks, medical labs, command centers, and the executive Prime Minister's chambers.
- **Other facilities:** Various smaller cloning facilities scattered across the planet. All targeted in the Imperial Era for orbital bombardment.

## Inhabitants

- **Kaminoans:** The native sentient species. Tall, slender, long-necked, large-eyed, hairless. Pale skin (some individuals' skin reflects polarized light). Highly intelligent; excellent geneticists. Cold and clinical in disposition; treat life as a product.
- **Notable Kaminoans (CW era):**
  - **Lama Su** — Prime Minister of Kamino. Polite diplomat; secretly negotiated with Tyranus and the inhibitor-chip alteration.
  - **Nala Se** — Chief Medical Scientist. Cold; runs the cloning program's medical operations. Adversarial to Shaak Ti throughout the war over clone treatment. Quietly aware of inhibitor chip purpose.
  - **Taun We** — Administrative officer; greeter for the original Kenobi visit in 22 BBY.

## The cloning operation

- **Genetic template:** **Jango Fett** — bounty hunter; killed at Geonosis 22 BBY. All clones share Jango's DNA.
- **Production process:** Genetic acceleration (clones age at approximately twice the rate of unaltered humans), behavioral conditioning, combat training, specialty assignment.
- **Training facilities:** Tipoca City clone training grounds — multi-stage cadet training including the **Citadel Challenge** (the canonical pre-graduation exercise where Domino Squad failed and Shaak Ti / Bric / El-Les presided as evaluators).
- **Inhibitor chips:** **Behavioral modification biochips** implanted in every clone's brain. Originally proposed (per Sifo-Dyas's request) as a safeguard against rogue Jedi and to reduce clone aggression. **Retooled by Tyranus and Sidious to enforce Order 66 — the Jedi-execution directive.** The chips are organic; they integrate with the clone's neural architecture; removal requires precise medical intervention.
- **The biochip conspiracy.** Late in the war (post-Ringo Vinda), clone trooper **Tup** (CT-5385) malfunctions and executes Jedi Master **Tiplar**. Sent to Kamino for examination. His fellow clone **Fives** (CT-5555) and the medical droid **AZI-3** uncover the chip during an unauthorized procedure. Tup dies; Fives investigates further; **Shaak Ti tries to extract the truth**; **Nala Se obstructs**; Fives flees to Coruscant to warn the Jedi; **Fives is killed before he can deliver the warning**. The conspiracy survives. Order 66 fires later as planned.

## Major Clone Wars events on Kamino

- **Battle of Kamino (mid-war).** CIS attack on the cloning facilities led by Asajj Ventress and General Grievous. Republic defense led by Shaak Ti, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Anakin Skywalker. Republic prevails; the cloning facilities are preserved.
- **The biochip conspiracy arc** (described above; spans late-war episodes).
- **Continuous clone production** throughout the war. New batches deploy regularly to replace casualties.

## Kamino-specific cultural traits

- **Clone individuality vs. Kaminoan management.** Canonical tension. Some clones develop names, hair styles, armor markings, distinct personalities; the Kaminoan management considers individuality a defect.
- **The maintenance clone "99."** A clone with developmental anomalies (older-looking, kinder-spirited, mobility-impaired) who serves as Tipoca City's maintenance clone. Beloved by the Domino Squad cadets. Killed during the Battle of Kamino while fighting alongside the cadets to defend the facility. Canonically the most affecting individual non-trooper clone of the era.
- **Clone Force 99** (the Bad Batch) named after maintenance clone 99 in tribute. See `wookieepedia_extracts/clone_force_99.md`.

## Voice and culture (for Director AI rendering)

### Kaminoan register
- **Cold, clinical, polite.** High-pitched. Refers to clones as "specimens," "products," "units." Uses scientific terminology rather than martial.
- **The Kaminoan accent.** Distinctive — slightly archaic constructions, formal vocabulary, soft consonants. The Director AI should let this be a marker.
- **Concealment of complicity.** Kaminoans canonically hide more than they reveal. Direct questions about clone treatment receive evasive technical answers.

### Clone trooper register on Kamino (cadet stage)
- **Eager, regimented, anxious to graduate.** Cadets defer to drill instructors and Jedi observers. Camaraderie within squads.
- **Awareness of Kaminoan distance.** Mature cadets know they are not loved by their creators. The Director AI should let this surface in private clone conversations.

### Shaak Ti's register
- **Compassionate authority.** See `wookieepedia_extracts/shaak_ti.md` — she is the canonical Jedi-clone interface here.

## Use in `weight_of_war_design_v1.md`

Kamino is the **canonical clone origin reference** for the design. Concrete uses:

- **Clone backstory grounding.** Player-character clones (regardless of unit) trace their origin to Kamino. Cadet training memories, Domino Squad-style training arcs, named drill instructors, the Citadel Challenge — all available content.
- **The maintenance clone 99 archetype.** Useful as an emotional anchor in clone-trooper content; canonical "the clone who didn't fit but mattered most."

## Use in `clone_wars_director_lore_pivot_design_v1.md`

- **Treat the biochip conspiracy as deep lore.** Players who investigate may find traces. The truth is heavily guarded.
