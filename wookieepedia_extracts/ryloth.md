## At a glance

**Ryloth** is the **Twi'lek homeworld** — Outer Rim desert and rocky planet, mining-rich, slavery-historical, occupied by the CIS during the Clone Wars and liberated in the canonical **Ryloth campaign** that earned **Mace Windu** the title "**The Hammer of Ryloth**." Capital: **Lessu**. Major cities **Nabat** and **Tann Province** also feature in canon. The Ryloth campaign demonstrates the Republic's commitment to liberating occupied worlds and showcases the Republic-Twi'lek-resistance cooperation that defines the canonical Mace-Cham Syndulla relationship.

For SW_MUSH purposes, Ryloth is **the canonical "occupied homeworld liberation" reference** — the model for a Republic counter-invasion that depends on cooperation with local resistance.

## Geography and physical character

- **Location:** Outer Rim Territories, Ryloth system, grid square R-17. The Slice region. Located along Mumble's Turnaround and the Death Wind Corridor hyperspace lanes.
- **Composition:** Rocky desert planet with significant mining operations. Tidally-locked rotation produces a permanent day-side and night-side, with most habitation in the temperate twilight band between.
- **Atmosphere:** Standard breathable in the twilight band; harsh elsewhere.
- **Major cities:**
  - **Lessu** — capital city, government seat, located on a plateau accessible only by a controlled bridge.
  - **Nabat** — significant city. Site of the Twi'lek hostage crisis during the Republic liberation campaign.
  - **Tann Province** — region containing the Syndulla family residence (Cham Syndulla's family).
- **Slavery history.** Ryloth has historical connections to the **Zygerrian Slave Empire** and the spice/Pyke trade. Twi'leks (especially female Twi'leks) have been heavily targeted by slavers across galactic history. This is canonical lore that shapes Twi'lek political consciousness.
- **Industries:** Mining (significant Techno Union operations); spice production; shadowport activity; Pyke Syndicate connections.

## Inhabitants

- **Twi'leks** — humanoid species with two head-tails (lekku) extending from the back of the skull. Multiple skin colors (blue, green, orange, red, etc.) reflecting genetic variation by clan/region. Native to Ryloth.
- **Notable Twi'leks (CW era):**
  - **Cham Syndulla** — Twi'lek resistance leader during the CIS occupation; later founder of the Free Ryloth movement during the Imperial Era. Earned Mace Windu's lasting respect during the Republic liberation.
  - **Senator Orn Free Taa** — Ryloth's Galactic Senate representative. Corrupt; politically loyal to Republic-then-Empire; canonically self-serving.
  - **Hera Syndulla** (Imperial-Era) — Cham's daughter; future *Ghost* captain. Out of CW project scope but worth noting as the future-arc seed.

## Clone Wars events

### CIS Occupation (~21 BBY)
- **Wat Tambor** (Techno Union foreman) occupies Ryloth as Separatist emir. Mining operations expanded under occupation; Twi'lek population subjected to occupation conditions; cities like Nabat used as staging grounds with civilians used as droid living-shields.

### The Liberation Campaign (~21 BBY)
- **Republic dispatches a multi-phase liberation:**
  - **Phase 1: Blockade run.** **Anakin Skywalker** and **Ahsoka Tano** break the CIS blockade above Ryloth, allowing Republic ground forces to deploy.
  - **Phase 2: Nabat hostage rescue.** **Obi-Wan Kenobi** and the **212th Attack Battalion** (Cody) land at Nabat and free Twi'lek civilians used as proton-cannon shields. Kenobi's diplomatic-priority approach — civilians first, then guns — shapes the campaign's character.
  - **Phase 3: Lessu / capital city liberation.** **Mace Windu** and his **187th Battalion** (under Commander Ponds) lead the assault on the capital city. The Republic forces ally with **Cham Syndulla's Twi'lek resistance**, who provide crucial local intelligence and supplemental forces.
  - **Wat Tambor's retreat order.** Dooku informs Tambor that he is no match for Windu and orders evacuation. Dooku then orders **TA-175** (Tambor's tactical droid) to bomb Lessu after the Separatist retreat, intending to leave only ruins. **Tambor is captured**; the bombing is partially carried out but stopped before Lessu is destroyed.
- **The "Hammer of Ryloth"** title — earned by Mace Windu through this campaign. Cham Syndulla canonically remembers Windu with deep respect; tells the tale of Ryloth's liberation for years afterward.
- **Result:** Republic victory. Ryloth liberated. Twi'leks rejoin the Republic. Cham Syndulla's resistance becomes a recognized Twi'lek national militia, the **Twi'lek Resistance** (later the **Free Ryloth movement** during the Imperial Era).

### Late-war secondary content
- **Trade liaison missions** during the late war involve Senator Orn Free Taa coordinating Republic operations through Ryloth.

## Voice and culture (for Director AI rendering)

### Twi'lek register
- **Lekku-expressive.** Twi'leks canonically use their head-tails for non-verbal communication; subtle lekku movements convey emotional state. The Director AI may include this as background detail.
- **Lekku of Ryloth dialect.** Twi'leks from Ryloth speak Galactic Basic with a distinctive accent; many also speak **Ryl** (Twi'lek language) and **Lekku** (silent gestural language using lekku movements).
- **Long memory of slavery.** Twi'leks canonically remember the slaver depredations of their species's history. Anti-slaver sentiment is foundational to Twi'lek identity.

### Cham Syndulla register
- **Resistance leader cadence.** Calm authority; tactical patience; deep loyalty to his people. Skeptical of Republic intentions initially (fears Ryloth will trade one occupier for another); converted by Mace Windu's actual conduct.

### CIS-occupier register
- Standard Techno Union / battle droid presence. Wat Tambor visits during the campaign.
