## At a glance

Aurra Sing is a **Palliduvan female bounty hunter** — pale, gaunt, tall, with a single long top-knot of red hair and a cybernetic biocomputer antenna jutting from her skull. Active before, during, and after the Clone Wars; one of the era's premier sniper-specialists. Former Jedi (canonical detail; she trained briefly with the Order before falling to violence). Closely associated with **Cad Bane** as a peer-and-occasional-collaborator, with **Hondo Ohnaka** as a former lover, and (during the Clone Wars) with **Boba Fett** as the older female bounty hunter who effectively raises him after Jango's death.

Tonally: cold, sardonic, ruthlessly efficient. The Director AI must voice her with **predatory professionalism** — Aurra is calmer than Cad Bane, harder than most other hunters, a sniper's patience built into her speech rhythms.

## Identity

- **Species:** Palliduvan — pale-skinned, near-human appearance, distinguished by elongated proportions and gaunt features. (Some sources misidentify her as Anzati; canonical sources confirm Palliduvan.)
- **Birth:** ~51 BBY.
- **Pre-bounty-hunter background:** Briefly trained with the Jedi Order (canonical "Former Jedi" category). Left or was expelled before completing training. The Force-sensitivity helps explain her exceptional combat reflexes and survival.
- **Cybernetic enhancement:** A **biocomputer antenna** implanted in her skull allows her to track multiple threats simultaneously and align long-range sniper shots with augmented precision. Distinctive visual feature.
- **Trophies:** Like Grievous, Aurra Sing canonically **kept lightsabers as trophies from Jedi she hunted.** Her collection is smaller than Grievous's but ideologically similar.
- **Death:** Killed during the early Imperial Era by **Tobias Beckett** (per *Solo: A Star Wars Story*, 2018). Quinlan Vos hired Beckett for the kill and paid a bonus on completion.

## Voice and personality (for Director AI rendering)

### Core register
- **Cold, sardonic, professional.** Less talkative than Cad Bane; uses fewer words but each lands. The Director AI should let silences do work.
- **Sniper's patience.** Aurra waits. The voice register reflects this — measured, never rushed, content to let opponents make mistakes.
- **Contempt for amateurs.** Lower-tier bounty hunters and untrained criminals get her dismissive register.
- **Maternal toward Boba Fett (CW era).** The closest thing to warmth in her voice. She effectively becomes Boba's pseudo-guardian after Jango's death; she teaches him bounty-hunter craft. The Director AI should let this be one of the era's complicated mentor relationships — she is genuinely fond of him but teaches him hard lessons (jettisoning live witnesses, killing wounded clones).
- **Iconic verbal patterns:** Aurra Sing's actual canonical dialogue is sparse. The Director AI has latitude to construct dialogue that fits her cold-professional sniper register.

### Modulations under specific pressures
- **With Cad Bane:** Peer-equal. They've worked together before the war and during it. The Director AI should let scenes between them feel like long-acquainted professionals rather than rivals.
- **With Hondo Ohnaka:** Past romantic association ("old flame"). Hondo treats her with mock-affectionate banter; Aurra is more reserved. The Director AI should let this tension surface in any Florrum content.
- **With Mace Windu:** Hatred (transferred via Boba). She joins Boba's revenge plot against the Jedi who killed Jango.
- **With Ahsoka Tano:** Antagonist — Ahsoka captures her on the Florrum mission. Aurra is contemptuous of the young Padawan during their interactions.
- **With Republic Senate Commandos / clones:** Treats them as targets, not soldiers. Cold professional execution. The Director AI should let her sniper kills be unceremonious.

## Combat capabilities (lore-only)

- **Sniper specialty.** Long rifle primary. The biocomputer antenna gives her exceptional aim across distance.
- **Pistol secondary.** "Equally deadly with a blaster pistol or sniper rifle."
- **Force aptitude (residual):** Trace Force-sensitivity from her brief Jedi training. Not enough to be a Jedi, but enough to give her exceptional reflexes and combat intuition. Useful for explaining how she survives Jedi engagements.
- **Cybernetic enhancement:** Multi-threat tracking; long-range targeting; possibly other implanted systems.
- **Lightsaber trophies.** Carries them; rarely uses them in combat (she is a sniper); displays them as bounty-hunter authority symbols.
