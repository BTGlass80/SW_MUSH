## At a glance

**Christophsis** is the **early Clone Wars Outer Rim battlefield** where Anakin Skywalker first met Ahsoka Tano as her assigned Master. Crystalline planet (surface canonically distinguished by enormous crystal formations growing from the ground in geometric patterns); Outer Rim Territories; Christoph system. Site of the **First Battle of Christophsis** (early CW) — a Republic victory against CIS occupation forces. Later, the **Quinlan Vos / Asajj Ventress arc** ends here when Ventress dies in the final Dooku confrontation.

For SW_MUSH purposes, Christophsis is **the era's first canonical battlefield** for new-Padawan content — the place where the Anakin-Ahsoka relationship begins. It is also a useful Outer Rim battle reference and a fixed campaign clock anchor for Ventress's death.

## Geography and physical character

- **Location:** Outer Rim Territories, Christoph system, grid square Q-16. The Slice region.
- **Composition:** Crystalline. The planet's surface canonically features enormous crystal formations — green and blue spires rising kilometers from the ground in geometric patterns. Cities are built among and around these crystal formations.
- **Climate:** Forested in some regions; rocky-crystalline elsewhere.
- **Atmosphere:** Standard breathable.
- **Major cities:** Crystalline construction integrated with the natural formations. Distinctive aesthetic.

## Clone Wars events

### The First Battle of Christophsis (early CW, ~22 BBY)
- **CIS occupation.** The Confederacy invades and occupies the planet under Asajj Ventress's overall command.
- **Republic counterattack.** **Anakin Skywalker** and **Obi-Wan Kenobi** lead Republic forces (501st and 212th Battalions, joint operation) to liberate the world. They establish a beachhead in the capital city; engage in heavy ground combat through the crystalline streets.
- **Yoda's diplomatic mission to Toydaria** runs parallel — Yoda arrives via Toyda to convince King Katuunko to grant Republic basing rights for Christophsis relief operations. Asajj Ventress confronts Yoda at Toydaria; Yoda defeats her in the canonical "small-Yoda-vs-droids" demonstration that wins Katuunko's loyalty.
- **Ahsoka Tano arrives.** Yoda assigns the new Padawan to Anakin; she is delivered to Christophsis to begin training under fire. **Their first meeting takes place on the battlefield.** Ahsoka calls Anakin "Skyguy"; Anakin calls Ahsoka "Snips." This canonical exchange is the founding moment of the era's defining Master-Padawan dynamic.
- **Republic victory.** The 501st and 212th drive the CIS off-world. Anakin and Obi-Wan share command credit.

### Late-CW use
- **The Quinlan Vos / Asajj Ventress arc terminates here.** During the climactic Dooku confrontation in the Vos arc, **Ventress sacrifices herself to save Vos** on Christophsis. Dooku kills her with Force lightning. Vos renounces the dark side. Ventress's body is later returned to Dathomir.

## Voice and culture (for Director AI rendering)

- **Christophsian register:** Educated cosmopolitan; the planet's pre-war population is wealthy and cultured. Christophsians are canonically humans of higher-than-average means.
- **Crystalline architecture sense.** Player content set on Christophsis should canonically convey the visual environment — shimmering crystal walls, refracted light, vertical geometric construction.
