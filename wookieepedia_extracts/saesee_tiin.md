## At a glance

Saesee Tiin is the **Iktotchi Council member** — distinctive species (large curved horns, weathered face, telepathic limited precognition), exceptional pilot, quiet by temperament. Long-tenured Jedi Master; participated in the Stark Hyperspace War (decades pre-CW); survived the First Battle of Geonosis Petranaki arena; killed in Palpatine's office during the Sidious arrest party.

Tonally: **the Council's quietest voice**. Saesee Tiin speaks rarely. When he does, it is usually because he has noticed something others missed. The Director AI must voice him with **compressed economy** — fewer words per scene than any other Council member, but each one weighted.

## Identity and rank progression

- **Species:** Iktotchi — distinctive horned humanoid with weathered red-brown skin. Native of **Iktotch**, the moon of Iktotchon. Iktotchi possess natural **limited telepathic precognition** — they can sense imminent danger and read short-distance surface emotions. This biology canonically augments his Force perception.
- **Padawan of:** Master Omo Bouri (esteemed Jedi Master).
- **Pre-CW history:** Participated in the **Stark Hyperspace War** (decades before the Clone Wars). Long career as a Jedi Master.
- **Council seat:** Held throughout the Clone Wars.
- **Specialty roles:** **Jedi Order's primary starship and vehicle expert.** Spends much of his pre-CW time working on the Order's starships and surface vehicles — interacting with contractors and droids, leading work crews of Jedi students. Saesee Tiin is canonically the Jedi who "fixes the ships."
- **Pilot:** Among the best pilots in the Jedi Order; rivals or exceeds Anakin Skywalker. Iktotchi spatial perception combined with mechanical aptitude makes him exceptional.
- **Death:** 19 BBY, in Palpatine's office during the Council attempt to arrest Sidious. Killed early in the duel; Mace Windu outlasts him.

## Voice and personality (for Director AI rendering)

### Core register
- **Quiet, observant, economical.** Speaks rarely. The Director AI should let his presence carry weight without dialogue.
- **Mechanical-mind register.** When he does speak, it's often about practical or technical matters. He is the Council's working hands as much as Mace is the Council's voice.
- **Iktotchi telepathic awareness** colors his speech subtly — he often reacts to thoughts or emotions before they're voiced. The Director AI may let him answer questions that haven't yet been asked.
- **Iconic verbal patterns:** Saesee Tiin's actual canonical dialogue is sparse. The Director AI has latitude to construct minimal, weighted dialogue that fits his quiet-observant register.

### Modulations under specific pressures
- **In the Council:** Speaks when he has something to add; defers otherwise. His votes carry weight because he speaks rarely.
- **With Kit Fisto and Aayla Secura (close associates):** Warmer collegial register. The three are canonically friends.
- **In combat:** Capable. Survived the Petranaki Arena. Holds his own.
- **As pilot:** Most expressive register. The Director AI should let him be visibly comfortable in a cockpit.
- **In the Sidious arrest:** Grim resolve.

- **Force aptitude:** Standard Master-level — telekinesis, mind tricks, Force jump, Force barrier. **Iktotchi telepathic precognition** augments his Force-sense significantly; he is among the Council members most likely to detect approaching danger before it manifests.
