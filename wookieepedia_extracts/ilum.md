## At a glance

**Ilum** is the **Jedi sacred ice world** — Unknown Regions arboreal/ice planet, primary source of **kyber crystals** for the Jedi Order, site of the canonical **Gathering ritual** where Jedi Initiates obtain their first kyber crystal for lightsaber construction. The Jedi Order's claim to Ilum dates back millennia. Imperial-Era strip-mining (18 BBY onward) destroys the planet's surface; out of CW project scope.

For SW_MUSH purposes, Ilum is **the canonical Padawan-Initiate ritual landmark** — central to any Force-sensitive player content involving lightsaber construction, kyber crystal acquisition, or initiate-tier tutorial chains. The Gathering ritual is one of the era's clearest tutorial templates.

## Geography and physical character

- **Location:** Unknown Regions, Ilum system, grid square G-7. Remote and hidden from standard hyperspace lanes.
- **Composition:** Ice planet with arboreal regions; permanent winter; jagged crystalline terrain.
- **The Crystal Caves:** The planet's defining feature. Massive ice caves containing veins of **kyber crystal** — the Force-attuned mineral used for lightsaber construction. The caves are sacred Jedi space; access controlled by Force-sensitive ritual rather than physical security.
- **The Gathering Chamber:** Ceremonial entry point to the Crystal Caves where Initiates enter to find their crystals. The entrance opens at sunrise and seals at sunset; Initiates have one day to find their crystal or fail the ritual.

## The Gathering ritual

A **canonical Jedi Order ritual** for Initiates. The procedure:

1. **Initiates travel to Ilum** as a group, typically led by Yoda or another senior Master, aboard the **Crucible** training ship (Professor Huyang's vessel).
2. **Initiates enter the Crystal Caves alone** at sunrise. Each must find their own crystal — guided by the Force, not by sight.
3. **The cave seals at sunset.** Initiates inside when the doors close are sealed in until next sunrise (or until they find their crystal). The pressure of the deadline tests their attunement.
4. **Each Initiate finds the crystal that calls to them** through the Force. The crystal "knows" its owner; the Initiate must trust the Force to guide them.
5. **Crystal acquired → lightsaber construction.** Following the ritual, Initiates construct their first lightsaber under Master Huyang's tutelage. The crystal's color emerges from the construction (most are blue or green; rarer colors signify rarer Force-attunement patterns).

## Ahsoka Tano's canonical Gathering

Per `wookieepedia_extracts/ahsoka_tano.md`: As an Initiate, Ahsoka traveled to Ilum and entered the Crystal Caves. She felt the crystals' presence as soon as the Jedi starship emerged from hyperspace. **Yoda told her she would know her crystal when she found it.** She entered the cave, struggled at first as the door began to seal at sunset, **calmed herself by recalling Yoda's teaching, and found her crystal's light.** She emerged with the crystal that became her first lightsaber.

This canonical episode is the model template for any Initiate-ritual content in player tutorials.

## Voice and culture (for Director AI rendering)

- **Sacred Jedi register.** Ilum is a holy place. Speech here is hushed, reverent, focused. The Director AI should let dialogue carry weight beyond the merely conversational.
- **Yoda's teaching cadence.** Yoda is the canonical Initiate-ritual leader. His inverted-syntax voice (per `wookieepedia_extracts/yoda.md`) shapes every Gathering.
- **Master Huyang's register.** Architectural-droid; ancient (he has been the Jedi Order's lightsaber-construction tutor for centuries). Patient, professorial, holds Initiates to high standards. Useful supporting NPC for any Ilum content.

## Use in `force_resonant_landmarks_design_v1.md`

Ilum is the **canonical primary Force-resonant landmark** — the first such site on the project's resonant-location list:

- **The Crystal Caves themselves are Force-active.** Initiates feel the kyber crystals' presence; the caves respond to Force-attunement; the door-sealing mechanism is canonically Force-driven, not mechanical.
- **Multiple difficulty levels.** Initiates can fail the ritual; the cave doesn't yield its crystals to those not ready. This makes Ilum a useful "you must earn this content" gate for Force-sensitive player progression.
- **Force-vision content.** Initiates often experience Force-visions during the ritual; player Initiates may receive personal visions tied to their character arc.

## Use in `padawan_master_system_design_v1.md`

Ilum is the **Initiate-to-Padawan transition landmark**. Pattern:

- **Initiate stage.** Player has trained at the Coruscant Temple under Master Huyang and the Initiate-class Masters (Yoda primarily).
- **Gathering ritual.** Player visits Ilum, finds crystal, constructs first lightsaber.
- **Padawan assignment.** Following the Gathering, the Initiate becomes a candidate for assignment to a Master.

The system might gate "advance to Padawan" content behind successful Gathering completion. This canonical bottleneck is structurally useful for player progression.

## Use in `cw_tutorial_chains_design_v1.md`

The **Gathering Tutorial Chain** templates as a five-beat sequence:

1. **Travel to Ilum.** Group voyage on the *Crucible*; introductions to Master Huyang.
2. **Yoda's teaching.** The Initiate-class Master's words about trusting the Force. ("You will know your crystal when you find it.")
3. **Entering the Crystal Caves.** Player passes through the Gathering Chamber alone.
4. **The trial of finding the crystal.** Time-pressured search; player must overcome panic and trust the Force.
5. **Construction and emergence.** Player constructs their lightsaber under Master Huyang's tutelage; the blade ignites; the player has graduated.

This is one of the cleanest, most discrete tutorial chains in the era's canon — perfect for Initiate-tier player content.
