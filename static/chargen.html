THE FORCE
Do you feel the Force? This choice affects gameplay significantly.

Force-Sensitive

You sense something beyond the physical world. Start with 2 Force Points and access to Force powers (Control, Sense, Alter). You must resist the temptation of the Dark Side.

Not Force-Sensitive

The Force is an energy field? You'll believe it when you see it. Start with 1 Force Point for dramatic moments. No Force powers, but no Dark Side risk either.
